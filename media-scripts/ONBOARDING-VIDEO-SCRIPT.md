# MoodFood — Onboarding Video Script
**Type:** Welcome / product intro  
**Duration:** 45–60 seconds  
**Audience:** New users, just downloaded the app  
**Purpose:** Warm welcome that captures the emotional promise of the app and builds excitement for completing the onboarding process  
**Production tool:** NotebookLM (see `VIDEO-STYLE-GUIDE.md` for visual and audio settings)

---

## SCENE 1 — Splash (0:00–0:06)
**Visual:** Full-screen food photo — vibrant, warm, colourful plate of food. The MoodFood logo fades in from the centre, glowing softly. The wordmark "MoodFood" appears below it.  
**Voiceover (warm, calm, personal):**
> *"What if dinner stopped feeling like a decision?"*

**Text overlay:** None — let the image breathe.

---

## SCENE 2 — The Problem (0:06–0:14)
**Visual:** A person staring blankly into an open fridge. Cut to a phone scrolling endlessly through food delivery apps. Cut to someone stressed at a desk at 6pm.  
**Voiceover:**
> *"Every evening, the same question. Too tired to think. Too hungry to decide."*

**Text overlay:** (centre screen, clean white) **6 PM. What's for dinner?**

---

## SCENE 3 — The Promise (0:14–0:26)
**Visual:** Transition to the MoodFood home screen — the hero recipe photo appears. A frosted chip reads "Cozy · 30 min". The user taps "Find tonight's dinner". A result card slides up smoothly.  
**Voiceover:**
> *"MoodFood is your personal dinner companion. Tell it how you feel. How much time you have. What sounds good tonight. And it picks one safe, perfect meal — just for you."*

**Text overlay:** Subtle caption: **One meal. Made for this moment.**

---

## SCENE 4 — Safety (0:26–0:32)
**Visual:** A ShieldCheck icon pulses gently over a recipe card. Allergen chips are highlighted in soft blue.  
**Voiceover:**
> *"Allergies and dietary needs are locked in from day one. They never get relaxed. Dinner is safe, every single time."*

**Text overlay:** **Your safety. Always first.**

---

## SCENE 5 — The Onboarding Invitation (0:32–0:46)
**Visual:** Quick montage of onboarding screens — the food photo hero changes with each section, the white bottom sheet slides into view, pill chips are tapped. The progress bar fills from left to right.  
**Voiceover:**
> *"It starts with a few questions. Your tastes. Your kitchen. How food makes you feel. The more you share, the better Moody gets. And every answer is yours — private, and changeable anytime."*

**Text overlay:** **28 questions. A lifetime of better dinners.**

---

## SCENE 6 — Welcome + CTA (0:46–0:57)
**Visual:** The "Food that meets your mood." welcome screen. The user taps "Build my food profile". A smooth transition to the first onboarding step.  
**Voiceover:**
> *"Let's build yours. MoodFood. Eat for the way you feel."*

**Text overlay:** (logo + wordmark centre screen, fade to black)

---

## NOTES FOR PRODUCTION
- Voiceover should be read at a **relaxed, conversational pace** — not rushed. Warmth over urgency.
- Pause 0.5 s at the end of each scene before the next cut.
- Background music should fade to near-silence under voiceover; let the VO carry the emotion.
- See `VIDEO-STYLE-GUIDE.md` for full visual and audio spec for NotebookLM.
